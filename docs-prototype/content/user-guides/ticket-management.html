Ticket Management Guide

The Ticket Management interface, available at tickets.html, allows users to track support requests and other operational tasks.

Creating a Ticket

  1. Navigate to the Tickets page.
  2. Click the "New Ticket" button.
  3. Fill in the required fields in the modal:
    • Title: A brief summary of the ticket.
    • Description: Detailed information about the issue or task.
    • Status: The current state of the ticket (e.g., Open, In Progress, Closed).
    • Priority: The urgency of the ticket (e.g., Low, Medium, High).
  4. Click "Save" to create the ticket.

Viewing and Editing Tickets

  • All tickets are displayed in a table on the main page.
  • Click the "Edit" button next to a ticket to open the editing modal and update its details.
  • Click the "Delete" button to remove a ticket.
